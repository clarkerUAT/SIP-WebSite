Welcome to the Home Inventory Magagment System

Display

The home Inventory Managment System is made with the couch shopper in mind. Are you tired of having to look through all your cupboards pantries fridges and freezers to find out what you need for groceries? With the Home Inventory Managment System you will no longer have to get off your couch. This system will track items and sort them by location. Then all this information will be stored in one location that you can accesss through any device. Devices that you can install in any location you wish will be able to scan the bar codes of items and automatically add and remove items from that location with one touch of a button.
